About Me

Hey, how's it going? My name is Daniel. I was born and raised in Orlando, Florida, and graduated from the University of Florida in 2016. After college, I moved to Atlanta, Georgia, to work on music videos for artists & brands including Spotify, Future, and T.I.

Recently settled in Washington, D.C., I am shifting into the tech industry to pursue a career in web application design and development.

I am a full stack web developer who thrives on work that is exciting, creative, and liberating. Continuously challenging myself every day brings me passion and inspires me to keep learning.
